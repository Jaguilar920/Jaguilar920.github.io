War Games!

Rules of The Game:

  • The Goal is 5,000 points!
  • There are 4 variations of enemies. The easier the enemy, the more commonly they will appear.
  • Each round, you will face one random foe.
  • Easy mobs are worth up to 500 points.
  • Medium mobs are worth up to 1,000 points.
  • Hard mobs are worth up to 2,000 points.
  • The boss is worth 3,000 points!

Choose how many rounds you wish to participate in. Note: You will have to survive until the entered number rounds has been completed.
